# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** [Insert Date]

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Reverse Audio ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Audio Recordings
- **What we collect:** Audio recordings you create using the App
- **How we use it:** To provide the core functionality of the App (reversing, applying effects)
- **Storage:** Audio files are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly share them

### 2.2 Device Information
- **What we collect:** Device model, operating system version, App version
- **How we use it:** To ensure compatibility and improve App performance
- **Legal basis:** Legitimate interest in providing a functional service

### 2.3 Usage Analytics
- **What we collect:** App usage patterns, feature usage, crash reports
- **How we use it:** To improve the App and fix bugs
- **Third-party service:** We use analytics services that may collect anonymized data
- **Legal basis:** Legitimate interest in improving our service

### 2.4 Purchase Information
- **What we collect:** Purchase history for premium subscriptions
- **How we use it:** To provide premium features and manage subscriptions
- **Third-party service:** Processed through Apple's App Store and RevenueCat
- **Legal basis:** Contract performance

## 3. Microphone Access

### 3.1 Permission
The App requests microphone access to enable audio recording functionality.

### 3.2 Usage
- Microphone is only accessed when you actively start a recording session
- We do not record audio without your explicit action
- We do not continuously monitor or record audio in the background

### 3.3 Control
You can revoke microphone access at any time through your device's Settings app.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Storage
- Audio recordings are stored locally on your device
- App preferences and settings are stored locally
- You can delete recordings at any time

### 4.2 Security Measures
- Data transmission (when applicable) is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
- We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information
- We do not store sensitive audio content on remote servers

## 5. Third-Party Services

### 5.1 RevenueCat
We use RevenueCat for subscription management:
- **Data shared:** Purchase information, device identifiers
- **Purpose:** Managing premium subscriptions
- **Privacy policy:** [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)

### 5.2 Apple App Store
Purchases are processed through Apple's App Store:
- **Data shared:** Purchase information as required by Apple
- **Purpose:** Processing subscription payments
- **Privacy policy:** [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/)

### 5.3 Analytics Services
We may use analytics services to understand App usage:
- **Data collected:** Anonymized usage patterns, crash reports
- **Purpose:** Improving App functionality and user experience

## 6. Data Sharing

### 6.1 No Sale of Personal Data
We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties.

### 6.2 Service Providers
We may share data with service providers who assist in operating our App, subject to appropriate confidentiality agreements.

### 6.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the public.

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

### 7.1 Access and Control
- View and delete your audio recordings through the App
- Manage subscription settings through your Apple ID account
- Control microphone access through device settings

### 7.2 Data Deletion
- Delete individual recordings through the App interface
- Remove all App data by uninstalling the App
- Contact us to request deletion of any data we may have collected

### 7.3 Opt-Out
- Disable analytics collection through App settings (if available)
- Manage subscription auto-renewal through App Store settings

## 8. Children's Privacy

### 8.1 Age Restrictions
The App is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

### 8.2 Parental Notice
If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete that information promptly.

## 9. International Users

### 9.1 Data Transfers
If you are located outside the country where we operate, your information may be transferred to and processed in other countries.

### 9.2 Legal Compliance
We comply with applicable data protection laws in jurisdictions where we operate.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

### 10.1 Updates
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or other appropriate means.

### 10.2 Continued Use
Your continued use of the App after changes to this policy constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
